About The Event

AWS User Group Kochi (AWSUGKOCHI) is proudly presenting our flagship annual event AWS Community Day Kochi 2024. Unveiling success stories, use cases & much more from industry leaders and community heroes and builders.

Where

Holiday Inn Cochin, an IHG Hotel

When

Saturday
14 December, 2024



AWS User Group Kochi


  • AWS User Group Kochi (AWSUGKOCHI) is a Kochi based group united by community-led learnings and diversified by the wide variety of tools and technologies closely knit with Amazon Web Services, cloud computing technology, AWS design implementation and servicing, high scalability / performance computing, production use cases of AWS, blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, Generative AI and much more.

  • AWS Community Days are community-organized cloud education events, featuring technical discussions and demos led by expert AWS users and industry leaders from around the world.

  • Community-based learning is the best way to enhance the concepts being taught by connecting with enthusiasts in person, thereby sharing first-hand experiences and familiar, accessible examples.

  • All skills levels are welcome to help accomplish our motto of learning, sharing and networking. Let's join hands to empower this tech community to make it bigger and better!

What to expect


  • Renew, revive & revitalize to exemplify your excitement about the work you do.

  • Learn industry trends and intricacies about innovative implementations from AWS experts

  • Harness the power of networking with 400+ AWS users, DevOps, developers & solutions architects.

  • Network with fellow AWS veterans & techies during breaks.

  • Novice to pioneer on relentless pursuit to learn can rejoice the AWS offerings & success stories under one roof.

  • Take helpful and valuable information back to business.

  • Gain visibility to get more publicity for your esteemed organisation.

  • Sporting chance for active participants to win AWS goodies.

F.A.Q

  • What is AWSUGKOCHI?

    AWS User Group Kochi (AWSUGKOCHI) is a Kochi based group united by community-led learnings and diversified by the wide variety of tools and technologies closely knit with Amazon Web Services, Cloud Computing Technology, AWS Design Implementation and Servicing, High Scalability / Performance Computing, Production use cases of AWS, Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, Generative AI and much more.

  • Who is conducting this event?

    The AWS Community Day is supported by AWS but independent of them. This event is organized and managed by AWS User Group Kochi with help from AWS and other User Groups from India.

  • How can I join AWSUGKOCHI?

    AWSUGKOCHI membership is through Meetup group, Join us via http://meetup.com/awsugkochi/

  • Can I present a topic in the Community Day?

    Yes, you are welcome to come forward and present topics. Please contact us and we will allocate a slot for you.

  • Is it free, how to register for the event?

    This is not a free event like our meetups. We will be charging a minimum amount from the attendees to cover the hotel expenses.

  • Can I cancel my registration?

    Cancelation is not allowed, we will not be processing any refunds. However, you can transfer the ticket to someone else. Contact us if you would like to transfer your ticket to someone else in case if you are not able to attend the event.

  • Do we have walk-in registration?

    No, all attendees should register in advance. Tickets will be closed by the second week of November based on availability.

  • Additional questions?

    We are always happy to hear from you. You can reach us for sponsorship enquires, topic proposals, ticketing, feedback or any general question on AWS Community Day Kochi 2024, please drop an email to info@awsugkochi.in
